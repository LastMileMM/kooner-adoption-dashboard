WK1–WK9

Key Takeaways

  • Overall adoption at 56.6% — 28.4% below the 85% target. 880 Kooner WOs vs 674 leaked (WK1–WK9 shown).
  • 3 stations on target: HMK4 (100%), HSL1 (96.4%), HCT2 (90.0%)
  • HLA2 is the largest leak source: 250 total leaked (37%), 25 in WK09 — ARMA accounts for 19 of those.
  • HDT3 highest raw volume: 133 leaked, 51.8% adoption. SFRM is the primary leaking DSP.
  • HPD1 critical: 2.9% adoption — 1 Kooner WO vs 34 leaked. Zero Kooner WOs in WK09.
  • WK09 leakage dropped to 80 — lowest since WK1. HAU1 improved sharply (2 leaked vs prior avg of ~10).

  3. HDY1: WK09 spike driven by TLGZ — new DSP needs Kooner onboarding ASAP.
  4. HTP2 / HHO3 / HDY1: All within 8 points of 85% — targeted push can close gap this month.
